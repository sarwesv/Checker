    How to Play

    • Pieces move diagonally toward the far side of the board.
    • Jump an opponent's piece to capture it — capturing is mandatory whenever it's available.
    • Chain jumps continue automatically: keep clicking landing squares until the jump sequence ends.
    • Reach the far row to crown a King, who moves and captures in all four diagonal directions.
    • Win by capturing every enemy piece, or by leaving your opponent with no legal move.
